"ABHYUDAY" : 3 YEARS IAS FOUNDATION COURSE

About the Course

The "ABHYUDAY" Program by Sankardev IAS Academy is a 3 years program for UPSC Civil Services Exam preparation. The course has been exclusively designed for college students with a 360-degree approach for the preparation of UPSC Civil Services Exam. The structure for IAS Foundation Course has been designed in such a way that students coming from the different backgrounds can easily understand the demand of Civil Services Examination. Anyone who has just got into college or are in 2nd year of their graduation are an ideal candidate for this program.

Advantages:

A Higher Secondary graduate student is a novice and has little or no idea about the structure and pattern of the exam. For such students, coping up with the preparation and college studies, in one year, simultaneously remains an intensive and herculean task. Three years foundation program has been designed by taking this factor into account. IAS preparation along with college studies has certain benefits:

  1. Early preparation saves quality time of your career. Generally, students start their preparation after graduation and further preparation takes 2 more years.
  2. Interaction with hundreds of toppers reveal that most of the toppers who qualified in their first attempt, started preparation in the college itself
  3. Cracking civil services in an early age gives you better prospects in your career ahead
  4. Having more time helps you can choose your optional more wisely and identify your weaker spots and rectify them as soon as possible
  5. More time for revision and inculcating an articulated way of answer writing

Structure of Course:

The course has been designed in a manner that will cover your entire syllabus in tandem with the college curriculum. The course has been divided into modules and covers one subject at a single time. The course will be structured as per the need of the course. It has been structured into 3 Stages:

First Stage: Integrated Preparation emphasizing basics with an integrated approach

Second Stage: Answer Writing Skill Development

Third Stage: Revision and Assessment through Prelims and Mains Test Series

The program will emphasize clearing the doubts and one on one interaction with faculty members. Students will have a privilege to clarify their doubts over the phone as well as on a personal basis. This program will also ensure paying individual attention will and regular counselling and motivational sessions by mentors

Programme Features

  • Highly recommended IAS Foundation Course for UPSC Preparation for College Going students
  • 3 Years IAS Foundation Course to help the Students to Prepare for IAS exam along with College Studies
  • An easy going IAS exam preparatory course that can be continued with college studies
  • Key focus is to build concepts and engage in practice so many assured delivery in examination can be achieved
  • Concept building from Basic to Advance
  • Current affairs class for updating with recent development
  • Complete coverage of UPSC Prelims and Main Examination Interview preparation with senior civil servants and IAS toppers
  • Updated and Complete Study Material that cover complete UPSC syllabus
  • Interactive classes where student can ask their Doubts
  • Regular interaction with IAS TOPPERS & Senior IAS Officers to boost confidence of aspirants. Idea is to keep kindling the dream of IAS and make them aware with Prospects of this service
