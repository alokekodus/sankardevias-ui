GS Foundation (Prelims & Mains)

General Studies is the core subject or area in which the UPSC IAS and other State Civil Services Exam evaluate a candidate for selection to the recruitment process.

It consists of a wide spectrum of knowledge base from basic to advanced concepts with their day-to-day life implications.

In order to excel any civil services exam, one must have a basic understanding (yet stronghold) of the General Studies syllabus prescribed by the commission for the purpose of selection procedure.

We at Sankardev IAS Academy have designed & tailored a comprehensive course on General studies intricately, as per the need of the UPSC and other State Public Service Commission. This course covers the syllabus for both Preliminary & Mains Examination.
