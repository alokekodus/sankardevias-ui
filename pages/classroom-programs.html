Features of Our Classroom Courses

Make learning fun

Learning is fun when the complex concepts are simplified and new information is explored and linked with practical experiences and story typed. The subjects and topics are arranged in a chronology with continuity to make learning fun and easily comprehensible so that every student from any educational background can understand and retain the very basic to advanced concepts for lifetime. Our classroom programs are designed to make learning fun through real life implications on the concepts and contents.

Comprehensive syllabus

Our syllabi and contents are crafted meticulously by the most experienced faculties in the country with deep analysis on the trend of way of question design by UPSC for IAS exam. The syllabus is designed in such a way covering all possible topics and concepts to enable the students in best-in-class preparation.

Quality delivery

Our motto is quality first and not quantity. Keeping in mind, the need of aspirants from different educational background and their information absorption capacity, our contents are crafted concisely and to the point for easy understanding.
We have the most experienced faculty in the field of IAS mentoring for the purpose of quality delivery. They do IAS mentoring at the most prestigious IAS coaching institutes in Delhi.

One to one interaction

Each and every student gets extra attention in the course of their preparation for IAS exam. Our faculties are available at the site before the commencement of classes, in the interval & breaks and after class till the institute opening time to guide and clear doubts of the enrolled students.

Extra benefits

Students enrolled at our institutes are beneficiary of lifetime access to the facilities offered at the time of enrollment till the goal is achieved.

Free study materials

Every student enrolled at Sankardev IAS Academy gets free concise and to the study materials based on the classroom delivery for easy reference and quick revision.

Special Sessions

We arrange special sessions on various issues and current affairs trending by experts from various fields from time to time. The persons delivering speeches are eminent personalities, working and ex-bureaucrats.
